Risk management options

Even with the best strategy, there will be situations where an asset loses value after you’ve bought it. Gunbot offers you the tools to deal with this.

With Double Up you can automatically average down an asset. Gunbot would buy more of an asset pair after the price dropped, and bring the average bought price down enough so it can still sell for profit.

Another option is to set a stop limit to sell assets off after a percentage of loss has been reached. If the whole market drops and you want out immediately, you can activate a panic sell mode.

Gunbot has a built in BTC surge protection: if there is too much buying pressure on Bitcoin - which usually affects all markets negatively - it prevents new buy orders from being placed as long as BTC keeps rising.
